Calorie Calculator

Please input your age, weight, height, activity level, and Gender into the form below and create a username. We will store this information along with your daily calorie maintence levels in order to provide you with a meal plan so that you may achieve your weight goal. The conversion chart below may help you estimate your metric measurements if you do not know them off the top of your head. If you would like to either lose or gain weight you can input your desired weight into the calorie calculator and it will give you a reccommended meal plan to achieve the weight you want.
